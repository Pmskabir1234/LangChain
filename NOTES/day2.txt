Prompts: The input instruction or queries given to model to guide it's output. Models output is heavily dependent on it.

Types:
    1. Textual
    2. Multimodal (Audio, Image, Video or any other form of input.)


Staic Prompts: Static prompts are fixed, pre-defined text instructions given to an AI.
    They remain constant across multiple interactions or queries, offering consistency.
    Best for repetitive tasks or when the required input is always the same.
Example: "Summarize this article" or "Translate this to Spanish."
They are simple to implement but lack adaptability to changing contexts.

Dynamic Prompts: Dynamic prompts, conversely, are generated or modified in real-time.
    Their content adapts based on user input, previous turns, or external data.
    This allows for highly personalized, context-aware, and interactive experiences.
Example: "Based on your prior interest in [topic], what are your thoughts on [related_item]?"
Dynamic prompts offer greater flexibility and sophistication, enhancing engagement and relevance.
